fitchwork



“Products powered by  novel fabrication and software.”
Development, UX



Fitchwork is a collaborative design studio working across architecture, fashion and product design. 

I worked with the designer over a few months to to bring his vision to life. We created an online shop as well as a ‘shopbot’, for product management and fulfillment.

Scroll to view



Overview

Being an architecht, Travis is as exacting as he is experimental. I worked closely with Travis to help shape his vision and bring it to life. Three separate versions of the website were created each with their own product selection system and layout until we reached a product that was ideally suited to both his vision and the customers’ needs. Shapeways even wrote about it.


Online Shop
The online shop was well beyond the limits of what a traditonal shopify site could support. It was build on top of shopify’s templating (liquid) but powered by a custom nodejs bridge and javascript to allow for product navigation, selection, customization.

Shopbot 

While working on the site, a custom shopify app was designed and deployed. Shopbot is the connector between shopify and shapeways. All the shapeways models and their possible material choices are loaded into the app in realtime and grouped with their shopify product.


Then, it is possible to select a material for each model. Custom sizing can be used
ex. ‘chair/seat/loung’ for a chair, ‘5/6/7/8’ for a ring, or ‘s/m/l’ for a necklace.

A job can be run to send customized orders from the store and all the necessary parts to shapeways with the correct customizations applied for fulfillment.




The Launch

One shopify site and two bespoke apps later, the site was complete. Travis continued to play around and experiment with me up until the afternoon of the launch to get everything just to his liking. It went off without a hitch.
